Internet Advertisements

Is blocking ads ethical? I do it. My gaze is (very) easily distracted, so I find much of the web unusable without aggressive content blocking.

Why block ads?

  • It undeniably makes a better web browsing experience.
  • Advertisers' goals and incentives are fundamentally against those of the user.
  • Ads are designed to manipulate the viewer into doing something they wouldn't normally.

Why not?

  • Writers, site maintainers, etc. who offer free products often rely on money from advertisers.
